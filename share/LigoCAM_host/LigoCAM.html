LIGO Channel Activity Monitor (LigoCAM) is a diagnostic tool for monitoring auxiliary channels. The utilities include locating a malfunctioning channel, graphic information of channel's time series and spectral data, and spectral change to understand various band-limited environmental disturbances of non-astrophysical origin.
